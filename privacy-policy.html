Use Common Sense in All Your Online Activities

It is always wise to consider that noPrivacy Policy can offer total privacy and security at all times. There arealways unethical advertisers, scam experts and hackers who are always trying toget victims. Therefore, you need to ensure that you apply common sense whencarrying out any activities online and understand the limits of privacy on theInternet.

It is important to note that e-mailing isessentially an insecure method of communicating. There are third parties thatmay be able to see your messages if they are not encrypted including greetingsyou send online.

Whenever you visit a chat room or write amessage on a bulletin board online, then advertisers will have access to youremail address. They will use this to send unsolicited emails containingcommercials or advertisements. So it is essential that you only publish youremail in situations when it is safe to. You should not offer your screen name,credit card information or password to people that you do not know. Beforeoffering such information, you should ask the person requesting for it toidentify himself/herself.
