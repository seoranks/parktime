Features in Park Time

Park Time is a fully featured web based time clock. We have all the features of a standard time clock, plus many more.

SCHEDULE

  • Make Your Schedule Faster
    Our scheduling software is quite easy to use so you can come up with a schedule in no time at all. This also ensures that there are no conflicts in the times and you can copy shifts, import schedules from your old system and much more.
  • Collective Scheduling
    You can allow your staff members to take care of trade management. This allows managers to save a lot of time that they would have been spending on making schedules since they can now designate these tasks.
  • No More Conflicts in Scheduling
    Conflicting schedules will now be a thing of the past since you get information on the staff members available currently. This allows you to correct any conflicts in case a member of staff is not available or there are conflicting shifts.
  • Automatic Scheduling
    You can create a schedule without any conflicts in shifts automatically and easily through the Schedule Wizard. After the wizard has made the schedule, you have the opportunity to go over it and make any changes before you broadcast it.
  • Unlimited Schedules
    There is no limit to the number of schedules you can create for your employees if you have more than one work team. You are also free to change the settings so that you can suit each group.
  • User friendly Scheduling Software
    The software is quite user friendly and there are visual cues, which means you can easily move an employee to another shift or change the date of a shift. This provides more time for you to spend on your business instead of using up all your time handling the schedule.
  • Multiple Schedulers
    If you have a large enterprise, you may have more than one person handling the schedules. This is not a problem when you are using Park Time since you can give each employee access to the schedules as needed.

Time Clock

  • Harmonize Your Calendar and Have It on the Go
    You can sync your Park Time softwarewith other applications such as Google Calendar, Outlook and iCal. This allowsyou to have your schedule anywhere and anytime.
  • Locations & Positions
    You can customize Park Time to suit your business depending on the departments you have. There are built-in location and structures so you can setup your account how you want it, for example, if you have more than one department, different business locations or just a single place.
  • Automatic Updates and Budgets
     Our scheduler automatically updates everything the moment you input something. This means you do not have to worry about losing your work if you have not saved it. You even get to see areas where there are discrepancies such as over or under staffing after preset intervals of time.
  • Multiple Views
    The employee scheduling software from Park Time allows you to choose, how you want to see your schedule. There are many views and you can choose depending on the duration or the graphical formats.
  • No More Late Arrivals
    Get rid of any excuses that touch on the schedule. Your employees have full time access to the schedules and can also see any shift changes. There are also text message or email reminders so that there is no risk of an employee forgetting a shift.
  • Everyone can Access
    You do not stop gaining access to Park Time once you leave the office. You can still continue accessing it through your mobile phone or tablet. The app is supported on smartphones and devices such as iPhone, iPad, Android and BlackBerry.
