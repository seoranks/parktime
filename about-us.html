About Park Time

We make the process of keeping time with payroll quite easy. We put a lot of emphasis on building technology that can help us collect and calculate time keeping data for processing payrolls.

Park Time is happy to offer pocket friendly and simple time keeping solutions for the payroll industry. We use technology to make timekeeping with payroll a lot easy. Our main driving factors are honesty, user friendliness and world class service. Our timekeeping services can be accessed through local service providers and not necessarily directly from our organization. Get in touch with us if you would wish to get more information about our time keeping solutions.

We are currently based in South Jordan, Utah. We have been offering timekeeping software to payroll service providers for more than ten years. We serve people who are in the United States and even Canada. Our company has been recognized as one of the fastest growing private companies in the United States over the last couple of years. We have also complied with all the regulations and standards set for such companies and we have been issued with certification.

Rather than process payroll and tax forms, we focus on offering timekeeping technology. We are continuously making work easy for payroll processors by improving how they collect and calculate timekeeping data. Park Time is not only meant for tech experts rather can be used by anyone. We have made extra efforts to include plug and play solutions so that there are few complexities that would normally accompany a timekeeping system.

The credit accorded to us is important. Therefore, we strive to stay committed in serving our clients through email, phone and even in the market place. As a matter of fact, we have many departments that were set up to serve our users. If you have any concerns or queries, we are always ready to help. We also look forward to helping your organization grow by offering you our technological and timekeeping services.
